With Apple Magic Mouse 2 Space Gray you swipe through web pages, scroll
through texts and navigate easily to your files. Thanks to the smooth,
multi-touch surface you control this mouse just like your smartphone.
Those who prefer to click in a traditional way use the one or two button
operation. The laser tracking mechanism combines optical technology with
the precision of a laser. Apple Magic Mouse 2 works smoothly on any
surface. And thanks to the wireless Bluetooth connection, a jumble of
cables is a thing of the past. Add a rechargeable battery and you have a
mouse that you can benefit from for years.
